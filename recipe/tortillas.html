Corn Cheese With Tortilla Crisps

Description

Dip homemade tortillas into sweet corn, crunchy capsicum and cheese which is all baked into a golden gooey mess.

Ingredients

  • 4 corn cobs, husks and silks removed
  • 1 bunch spring onions, thinly sliced with white and green parts seperated
  • 1 red capsicum, halved, deseeded and finely chopped
  • 1 cup whole egg mayonnaise
  • 2.5 cups shredded mozzarella
  • 0.5 tsp smoked paprika
  • 5 flour tortillas
  • 5ml olive oil cooking spray

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C/200°C fan-forced. Line 2 baking trays with baking paper.
  2. Remove the kernels from corn cob and discard the cob
  3. Combine corn, white parts of onion, capsicum, mayonnaise and mozzarella in a 1.5L (6-cup) capacity baking dish. Sprinkle with paprika. Bake for 25 minutes or until golden.
  4. Stack tortillas and cut into 8 wedges. Arrange tortillas, in a single layer, on baking trays. Spray with oil. Bake in the last 10 minutes of corn cooking time or until golden and crisp.
  5. Scatter corn with green parts of onion. Serve with tortilla crisps.
