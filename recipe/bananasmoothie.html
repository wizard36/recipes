Layered dragonfruit and banana smoothie recipe

Description

Sure to become your new fave smoothie recipe, these layered smoothies are ready in 10 minutes and feature banana, spinach, yoghurt and dragon fruit.

Ingredients

Spinach Layer

  • 1/3 cups Greek-style yoghurt, stirred until smooth
  • 2 bananas
  • 1 cup baby spinach leaves
  • 1 cup ice cubes
  • 2 tbs almond milk

Dragon Fruit Layer

  • 1 cup frozen pink dragon fruit chunks
  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup ice cubes
  • 1/4 cup almond milk

Steps

  1. To make the spinach layer, place all the ingredients in a blender and blitz until smooth. Divide mixture between 2 x 300ml capacity glasses. Place in freezer until needed.
  2. To make the dragon fruit layer, place all the ingredients in cleaned blender and blitz until smooth.
  3. Spoon yoghurt over spinach layer. Top with dragon fruit mixture. Serve.
